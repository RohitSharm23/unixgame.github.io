About the Game

Battle Cars is a fun monster truck online confrontation game. You will have the opportunity to drive a crazy kart with various armor functions against other monster trucks on the field map, launch armor-piercing bullets and To avoid the attacks of other players, you first need to collect various props on the track, and then use these props to ensure your survival during the confrontation. Are you ready to accept the challenge?

How to Play

Mouse or tap to play.
