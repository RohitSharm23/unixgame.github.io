About the Game

The player must escape from her enemy and avoid all the obstacles and traps that are found along the way, and escape with the gold idol. From the icy caves and the narrow ledges of the ravines to the dangerous ice skates, the Frozen Shadows map adds more depth and excitement to the classic Temple Run.!

How to Play

Keyboard Move - AD or Left/Right arrow keys Jump - W or Up arrow key Slide down- S or Down arrow key Mobile Move and turn - swipe left / right Jump - swipe up Slide - swipe down
