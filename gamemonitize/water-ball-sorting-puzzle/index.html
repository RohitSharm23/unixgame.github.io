About the Game

Sorting Puzzle Game is a fun, relaxing, and brain-boosting puzzle game designed for players who enjoy logical challenges and color sorting gameplay. In this casual puzzle game, your main task is to sort different items, colors, or objects into the correct containers using smart moves and strategic thinking. Each level offers a unique puzzle that tests your problem-solving skills in an enjoyable way.
