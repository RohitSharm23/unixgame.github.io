About the Game

This is a tricky and relaxing puzzle game with lots of funny levels!Deep in the ocean lives a beautiful and mysterious tribe of mermaids. One day, an evil ocean overlord captures the mermaids and imprisons them in a dark underwater prison. As a brave underwater explorer, your mission is to dive into the depths, rescue the trapped mermaids, and restore harmony and tranquility to the ocean.

How to Play

Try moving the pins by your finger to help Mermaid keep away from hidden trap and escape from the chasing Piranha. Little Mermaid need your rescue and it all depends on you! Don’t hesitate any more and take advantage of your wisdom to save the Merma
