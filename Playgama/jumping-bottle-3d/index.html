Can you complete all the levels? Get ready for incredible adventures with Jumping Bottle 3D!

How to Play

🚀 In "Jumping Bottle 3D" your task is to complete all levels by throwing the bottle over obstacles.
The main rule is not to touch the floor with the bottle! 📱 The controls are very simple:

1. Tap the screen to make the bottle jump and turn over.
2. Double tap the screen to make the bottle taller and spin faster.
💰 Collect as many gold caps as you can to unlock amazing new bottle skins! Milk, Coca-Cola, poison and other bottles are waiting for you.
🏆 Complete exciting levels and get cool rewards! Can you complete all the missions on the first try?"
