Traffic Rider

About the Game

Experience high-speed motorcycle racing with first-person view, real bike sounds, and career missions in Traffic Rider. Ride, overtake, and dominate the highway! A first-person endless motorcycle racing game featuring career mode, realistic bike sounds, and high-speed highway action. Traffic Rider is another masterpiece from the creators of Traffic Racer, bringing a fresh and immersive take on endless racing—this time from the seat of a powerful motorbike. Featuring a first-person camera view, realistic bike physics, and real-life recorded engine sounds, the game delivers an authentic riding experience with smooth arcade-style gameplay. Race through endless highways filled with traffic, overtake cars at high speed, and earn rewards to upgrade or unlock new motorcycles. The full career mode offers over 90 challenging missions, pushing your riding skills to the limit across varied environments with dynamic day and night cycles. Whether chasing high scores or mastering wheelies, Traffic Rider keeps the adrenaline flowing with every ride. Features • Immersive first-person motorcycle camera view 🏍️ • 34 powerful motorbikes to unlock and ride 🛵 • Realistic engine sounds recorded from real bikes 🔊 • Detailed environments with dynamic day and night cycles 🌗 • Career mode featuring 90+ challenging missions 🎯 • Online leaderboards and 30+ achievements 🏆 • Support for 19 different languages 🌍 Tips • Ride faster to earn higher scores ⚡ • Overtake traffic closely at 100+ km/h for bonus cash 💰 • Drive in the opposite lane for extra rewards 🔄 • Perform wheelies to gain additional score and money 🎉

How to Play

Controls (PC) • Arrow Keys – Control the bike 🧭 • Mouse – Menu navigation 🖱️ • R – Horn Controls (Mobile/Tablet) • Touch controls for steering, acceleration, and braking 📱
